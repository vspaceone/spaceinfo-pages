This computerized system associate with the spaceinfo displays
has been seized pursuant to an order issued by a U.S District Court.

A federal grand jury has indicted several individuals and entities allegedly
involved in the operation of the spaceinfo monitors and related systems
charging them with the following federal crimes:

Conspiracy to Commit Racketeering (18 U.S.C. 1962(d)), Conspiracy to Commit
Copyright Infringement (18 U.S.C. 371), Conspiracy to Commit Money
Laundering (18 U.S.C. 1956(h)), and Criminal Copyright Infringement (18
U.S.C. 2, 2319; 17 U.S.C. 506).
